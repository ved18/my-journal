This Bangalore trip was more of catching up with one of my best friends. Ended up having some of the best food in Koramangala. Would definitely recommend going if you are close by. Having such amazing food after being outside India for a year, it couldn't get any better.

Ranking in the order I liked them.

### 1. [<u>My Marathi - Taste of Maharashtra</u>](https://maps.app.goo.gl/srbKwo5cQm2a1qdt9)

I visited this place twice in my trip as I could not try everything in the first visit. This is the closest you could get to Maharashtrian food in Bangalore.

title: "First Impressions of Bangalore"
date:   2026-03-18 13:20:00 +0530
categories: travel bangalore
---

Bangalore, the Silicon Valley of India, welcomed me with its perfect blend of tradition and technology. The city's pleasant climate surprised me - no wonder it's called the Garden City!

The traffic was exactly as everyone warned - chaotic but somehow organized. The food scene is incredible, from street-side dosa stalls to high-end restaurants.

What struck me most was the energy of the city. Everyone seems to be building something, whether it's a startup, a community, or just their dreams.

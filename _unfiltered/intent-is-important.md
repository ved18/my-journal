# Intent is Important

Met my first manager from the corporate world today. One of the best managers I've had. The meeting was good. I didn’t have anything specific in mind, but ended up getting good advice on entrepreneurship. Another person telling me that the idea need not be niche. In today's world, most things have already been implemented. Find a problem and, if you think it needs to be solved, work towards it. Idea is good, business is not — LEAVE. The idea is bad, but business is good — go for it.

Each day in life is making me realize —

Intent is extremely important!

Until you keep something in mind, you won't be able to know if it will work. You miss 100 percent of shots you don't take.
